Journey Timeline

旅程時間線

Follow our 3-day transformative journey through history and healing practices

Day 1

La Vang - Quang Tri Citadel - Khe Sanh

Morning visit to La Vang Holy Land, followed by solemn remembrance at Route 9 National Cemetery. Afternoon exploration of Khe Sanh Combat Base with historical context. Evening meditation with floating lantern ceremony on Thach Han River.

Healing Practice: Evening mindfulness meditation & lantern ceremony for reflection

Day 2

Con Co Island Healing Retreat

Boat journey to Con Co Island - a strategic military outpost turned peaceful sanctuary. Visit island monuments, historic lighthouse, and pristine beaches. Practice sunrise Qigong on the beach and afternoon meditation in ancient forests.

Healing Practice: Beach Qigong for energy flow & forest meditation for grounding

Day 3

Vinh Moc Tunnels - Hien Luong Bridge - Integration

Explore the incredible Vinh Moc tunnel network where families lived underground during wartime. Visit Hien Luong Bridge at the former DMZ border. Closing integration meditation session to process the journey's lessons and insights.

Healing Practice: Integration meditation & farewell ceremony for closure

Frequently Asked Questions

常見問題

Answers to common questions about our DMZ Healing Journey

What makes this DMZ tour different from others?
Our DMZ Healing Journey uniquely combines historical site visits with mindfulness meditation and Qigong practices led by certified guides. It's not just a historical tour, but a transformative healing experience designed to help process emotions and find peace.
Is this tour suitable for Vietnam War veterans?
Yes, we have a special Veteran Reconciliation Journey designed specifically for veterans and military families, with trauma-informed guidance, sensitive healing practices, and optional counseling support.
What is included in the tour price?
Includes: All transportation, 3-star accommodation, all meals, English-speaking guide, meditation & Qigong sessions, all entrance fees, travel insurance, and healing materials. Excludes: Personal expenses, tips, and optional activities.
How physically demanding is the tour?
Moderate fitness level required. Includes walking on uneven terrain at historical sites. Meditation and Qigong sessions are gentle and suitable for all fitness levels. We can accommodate most mobility requirements with advance notice.
